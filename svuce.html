About


Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering (SVCE) Tirupati is an engineering college in Andhra Pradesh, India, established in 2007. It's affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Anantapur (JNTUA), and recognized by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). SVCE aims to develop global leaders with strong technical skills and ethical values, offering a variety of engineering programs, including B.Tech, M.Tech, and MBA. The college also boasts a vibrant campus life with various clubs and activities.


Visit Official Website - https://svuce.edu.in/

History

SVUCE, a constituent and Autonomous College of Sri Venkateswara University, Tirupati, was established in 1959 and started functioning from 10 August 1959.[2] The foundation stone for the college Main Building was laid on 13 October 1959 by Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru, the first Prime Minister of India. It was declared open on 21 November 1968 by Sri. K Brahmananda Reddy, the then Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh. From the year 1977–78, the names of BE and ME were changed as B.Tech. and M.Tech. The institution achieved autonomous status in 2005. SVU Secured 63rd Rank out of 100 Ranks, out of about 850 Central, State, Deemed, Private Universities, Central Institutions in India. S.V.University stands 13th position in Research Component.

Sri Venkateswara University, Tirupati also stands 186th Rank in the BRICS World Ranking – 2016.

Alumni Association

The college alumni has presence all over the globe. The alumni association of SVUCE was started in 1965. It was formally registered in the year 2001. The association is currently presided by B. Gangi Reddy. There are hundreds of SVUCE alumni based in western countries such as the US, Canada, Australia, and UK. This powerful alumni network has led to job opportunities for many graduates. The official Alumni Network of SVU College of Engineering website is svucealumni.org.

Key Features and Highlights


Vision:

- The Vision of Sri Venkateswara University College of Engineering is to be the leader in the creation and development of globally competitive human capital in Engineering Education for Technological, Economical and Social Enrichment of the Society, through its open and flexible Academic Programs.

Mission:

=> To be recognized as a premier institution offering Engineering Education programs, training human resources oriented to problem solving and system development.

=> To carry out research in Engineering and Technology relevant to all segments of society.

=> To assume leadership in sustainable technological growth of the Indian society.

=> To be a natural destination for excellence and diversity in thought and practice.

Placement Support:

- SVUCE offers placement training and support, with reputed companies visiting the college for recruitment.

Campus Life:

- SVUCE has various clubs and activities, including an Arts & Crafts Club and sports programs.

MBA Program:

- SVUCE offers an MBA program that aims to develop students' business acumen and leadership skills.

B.Tech Admissions:

- UG (B. Tech) admissions are conducted through the Andhra Pradesh state government's common entrance test (EAPCET).

Lateral Entry:

- Engineering Diploma holders can be admitted into Lateral Entry (II year B.Tech Courses) based on their merit at ECET (FDH).
